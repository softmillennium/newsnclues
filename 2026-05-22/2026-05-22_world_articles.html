MOSCOW, May 22. /TASS/. By "the spirit of Anchorage," Russia means the existing atmosphere of trust between the presidents of Russia and the United States, Vladimir Putin and Donald Trump, which allowed to outline the basic parameters of Ukrainian reconciliation, Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Sergey Ryabkov told Vedomosti in an interview. "By 'the spirit of Anchorage' - if you insist on this term - we imply the atmosphere of trust that emerged between the presidents of Russia and the US, which, in the context of last year's Alaska summit, allowed to shape the basic contours of the Ukrainian peace process at the level of the two states' governments.
