In his social media post, Isaacman said that, if the Project Athena document becomes public, “I will stand behind it,” adding that he believes “there are many elements of the plan that the space community and NASA would find exciting.” More broadly, Isaacman has suggested he intends to enter the NASA job with an open mind and adjust course based on feedback.
