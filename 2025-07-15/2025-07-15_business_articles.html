Russia is staring down a long-term economic threat that could outlast both the war in Ukraine and Western sanctions: a deepening demographic crisis. On Tuesday, Labor Minister Anton Kotyakov underscored the scale of the problem during a meeting with President Vladimir Putin. Kotyakov said the number is required to replace 10.1 million people who will reach retirement age, and 800,000 new jobs.
