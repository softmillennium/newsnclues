Bitcoin's incentive structure relies on miners being compensated for their work through block rewards and transaction fees. “As block rewards shrink, more weight falls on transaction fees,” Pierre Samaties, chief business officer at the Dfinity Foundation, told Cointelegraph. “If usage does not grow, that base thins, and the guarantees weaken.
