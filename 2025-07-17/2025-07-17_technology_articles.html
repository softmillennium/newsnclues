According to the company's press release, the Rocket 1628A and 1528D NVMe Switch Adapters let you directly plug up to eight PCIe devices into one PCIe 5.0 x16 or PCIe 4.0 x16 slot, or up to 32 NVMe drives via a UBM-compliant backplane. The combined company will offer tools that span the full design spectrum, from transistor-level chip design (Synopsys) to physical system analysis and simulation (Ansys).
