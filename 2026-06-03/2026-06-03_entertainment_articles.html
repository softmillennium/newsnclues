He teamed with in-house lyricist Cynthia Biggs for a fruitful run that reaped songs such as LaBelle's No. Wansel was also a producer on Lou Rawls' 1977 album Unmistakably Lou, for which the singer won a Grammy for best male R&B vocal performance. Wansel's work has also been sampled by various hip-hop artists, including Kendrick Lamar, Wiz Khalifa, Lil Kim, Drake and J. Cole.
