While a Bank of Canada-run digital currency comes with its own potential drawbacks, the policy vacuum leaves our market to foreign alternatives. Canada's top financial regulatory agencies are aware of this, collaborating on a framework for stablecoins. For this effort to succeed, our regulators should start by legally defining stablecoins as a payment tool, bringing them under the watch of an updated Retail Payment Activities Act.
