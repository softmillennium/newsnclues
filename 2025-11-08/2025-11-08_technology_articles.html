At their core, both start with a unique idea and a small team, naïve and determined enough to believe they can pull it off. The risks are high and many never make it. In both cases, we started by ideating on how to make the idea real — sketching, testing, and adjusting until we found something that resonated. In both startups and films, you must hire quickly, fire carefully, and align people around the mission.
