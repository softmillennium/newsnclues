Green Art Wall returns as innovation takes centre stage at Nitto ATP Finals
Source: {'href': 'https://www.atptour.com', 'title': 'ATP Tour'}    Published: 2025-11-08 13:08:55

With the Green Art Wall Project, the Nitto ATP Finals has become inextricably linked to the city of Turin. A mural that promotes sustainability and respect for the environment, it is an artistic creation that, in addition to contributing to urban regeneration, helps to improve air quality. The mural has been created using special Airlite paints capable of purifying the air and concretely contributing to the improvement of the surrounding environment. This work forms part of a broader initiative, the Nitto ATP Finals Torino Green Project, launched in 2023 to promote sustainability and innovation in the city of Turin. The Green Art Wall is an expression of gratitude from the Nitto ATP Finals towards the citizens of Turin: a legacy that the event leaves to the city, marking the culmination of its first five-year presence in Turin. The mural, created by the Turin-based artist MrFijodor and hosted on the wall of the Istituto Comprensivo Leone Senigaglia, tells the story of the connection between sustainability and tennis, a bond founded on shared values such as commitment, growth, and respect.
