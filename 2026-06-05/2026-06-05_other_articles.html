Following two major 2025 announcements of possible extraterrestrial life — on exoplanet K2-18 b in April and in the Cheyava Falls rock on Mars in September — we surveyed the astrobiology community to capture the spread of expert opinion. 'Supernatural' Era Comes to an End With the Release of New Chapter
Source: {'href': 'https://movieweb.com', 'title': 'MovieWeb'}    Published: 2026-06-05 05:23:21

From 2005 until 2020, TV viewers got to follow Sam and Dean Winchester on their everlasting battle against demons, vampires, werewolves, and countless other monsters in Supernatural on The CW (after one season on The WB). Under the watch of creator Eric Kripke, who also created Prime Video's The Boys, Jared Padalecki (Sam) and Jensen Ackles (Dean) helped lead one of the most popular shows on television, thanks to the pair's unparalleled brotherly chemistry and their use of humor, horror, and deeply emotional family moments. The fandom got a small taste of new Supernatural material through its 2022 spin-off, The Winchesters, centered on Sam and Dean's parents (John and Mary) in their younger years. However, the series only lasted one season before its cancellation, bringing back the void left by Sam and Dean's exit from the small screen almost six years ago. Dynamite Entertainment (as reported by Collider) added a new chapter to the Supernatural story with a 10-issue comic series. While plot details are still being kept largely under wraps, reports tease Sam and Dean facing off against "one final foe (for now!)." This run is set between the first two seasons of the original show, after Jeffrey Dean Morgan's John Winchester traded his life for Dean's with Frederic Lehne's Azazel (then known as Yellow Eyes). Recent issues have also taken a diversion from Sam and Dean's usual enemies, trading in ghosts and demons for killer robots and other kinds of monsters they have never seen before. After Ackles was introduced as Soldier Boy in Season 3 of The Boys, 2026's Season 5 (the show's final season) gave him a full reunion with Padalecki and Misha Collins after they spent 12 years working together on Supernatural. Following that episode and the new comic run for Supernatural, many are sure to wonder whether the series could ever be brought back for a 16th season. While the Season 15 finale killed off both Sam and Dean and sent them to heaven, after both cheated death multiple times in the show's long run, anything is possible when looking at them potentially being brought back to life.
