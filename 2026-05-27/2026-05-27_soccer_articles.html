Club Tijuana to host Iran's training base for FIFA World Cup 2026
Source: {'href': 'https://thenewsmill.com', 'title': 'The News Mill'}    Published: 2026-05-27 07:48:42

TNM (With ANI Inputs) | Published On: May 27, 2026 ANI Photo | Club Tijuana to host Iran's training base for FIFA World Cup 2026 Club Tijuana, a football club based in the Mexican city of Tijuana, has expressed “great pride” in serving as the training base for Iran's national football team during the FIFA World Cup 2026. The Iranian team shifted its training base from the United States to Mexico amid ongoing geopolitical tensions. Iran will remain in Tijuana, near the US border, while playing its World Cup matches in the United States during the tournament, scheduled to take place from June 11 to July 19 and jointly hosted by the US, Mexico, and Canada. In an official statement posted on X, Club Tijuana welcomed the decision, describing the city as “welcoming, hospitable and passionate about sports.” The translated statement read: “We have been notified with great pride by FIFA that Tijuana will play a part in the 2026 FIFA World Cup, serving as the base camp for the Iranian National Team. Tijuana is a city with open doors–friendly and hospitable; a city that embraces the world through its culture, its people, and its passion for sports.” The club further highlighted the readiness of Baja California and Tijuana to welcome visitors during the tournament and showcase the region's culture and international reputation on and off the pitch. The statement continued: “The 2026 FIFA World Cup will also come alive in Baja California, and we are ready to welcome the world, share the essence of our border region, and demonstrate why Tijuana stands today as an international benchmark–both on and off the pitch.” Despite relocating their training base, Iran will play all three of their group-stage matches in the United States. The team will face New Zealand in Inglewood, California, on June 15, Belgium on June 21 at the same venue, and Egypt in Seattle on June 26.
