Who has completed the ‘Sunshine Double'? Sinner aims to join exclusive list in Miami
Source: {'href': 'https://www.atptour.com', 'title': 'ATP Tour'}    Published: 2026-03-29 00:53:47

Jannik Sinner will aim to add his name to an exclusive list in ATP Tour history on Sunday at the Miami Open presented by Itau. Find out more about this coveted feat below. Seven different men have completed the Sunshine Double at least once: Jim Courier (1991), Michael Chang (1992), Pete Sampras (1994), Marcelo Rios (1998), Andre Agassi (2001), Roger Federer (2005-06) and Novak Djokovic (2011, 2014-16). Sinner is bidding to become the first man to complete the Sunshine Double since Roger Federer in 2017. Sinner is bidding to join Sabalenka as a Sunshine Double winner in 2026 after the WTA World No. 1 overcame Coco Gauff on Saturday in Miami. The first time it happened was in 1994, when Sampras and Steffi Graf each triumphed in Indian Wells and Miami. In 2005, Federer and Kim Clijsters each won both events, while in 2016 Djokovic and Victoria Azarenka both secured Sunshine Double glory.
