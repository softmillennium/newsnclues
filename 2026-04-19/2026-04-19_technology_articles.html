The Roborock Qrevo Curv 2 Flow is an impressively powerful mop, but it has a limited reach. The biggest update is the new mopping system, which Roborock calls the “SpiraFlow Roller Mop.” It's actually quite slick, featuring a wide, 10.5-inch roller mop (that looks not unlike a paint roller) that spins at 220 rpm and exerts an impressive amount of downward pressure.
