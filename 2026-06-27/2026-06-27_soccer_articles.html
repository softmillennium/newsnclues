By contrast, the U.S. head coaching job was seen by many in the soccer world as a step down in prestige. The 2026 World Cup, hosted on home soil, represented a once-in-a-generation opportunity to take a big step toward changing that status quo. "The Argentinian player takes every call-up as though it's the World Cup Final and as though it's his last ever opportunity."
