Italian Lorenzo Musetti controls his own Nitto ATP Finals qualification destiny this week in Athens as the 2025 ATP Tour season enters the final week of the regular season. Musetti slipped outside the cut last week in Paris when Felix Auger-Aliassime moved to eighth by reaching the final. Should 23-year-old Musetti join new World No. 1 Sinner in Turin, it will be the first time that two Italians have qualified for the season finale, which this year offers more than $5 million to an undefeated champion. Musetti, who fell to countryman Lorenzo Sonego in his Paris opener, has a bye in the first round of the Vanda Pharmaceuticals Hellenic Championship in Athens.
