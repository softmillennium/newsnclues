London's Leicester Square played host to the UK premiere of 'F1 The Movie' and plenty of stars showed up on the red carpet, ahead of the film's official international release on June 25. Brad Pitt was in attendance, the American superstar sharing top billing with Damson Idris in the movie. It was a balmy night in London, with the fans turning to try and catch a glimpse of the movie's stars, along with plenty of A-listers including superstar actor Tom Cruise.
