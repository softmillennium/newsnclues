Red Bull and Racing Bulls have announced a joint season launch alongside Ford ahead of the 2026 campaign. At the United States home of Ford in Detroit, Michigan, on January 15, the two teams will unveil their respective liveries for the next generation of cars, a season in which Red Bull Ford Powertrains will debut as both squads' power unit manufacturer. Laurent Mekies, CEO and Team Principal of Red Bull Racing, said: "The launch of the Red Bull Ford Powertrains era represents not only a bold step into the future, but a powerful expression of what's possible when world-class engineering, innovation, and passion come together. “To see the energy, precision, and scale behind this project is inspiring. We're incredibly excited to begin this new chapter, driven by the same determination and excellence that defines both Ford and Red Bull." Jim Farley, President and CEO of Ford Motor Company, added: "Since we announced our return to F1 with Red Bull, the Ford team have been working night and day to get ready for 2026. But this is about so much more than just the racing. What we will learn together with Red Bull will define the technologies of the future and that is what excites me most about this relationship."
