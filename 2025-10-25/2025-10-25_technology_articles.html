India is home to more than 700 million smartphone users and has seen a sharp rise in digital payments, which now account for over 99% of all transactions in the country. Car sales have climbed in tandem, reaching 4.73 million vehicles in 2024, up from 3.87 million a year earlier — a 5.2% year-on-year increase and an all-time high.
